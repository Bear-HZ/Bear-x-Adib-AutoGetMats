Bear x Adib AutoGetMats v1.9.1

Document Author: Bear (Swapnil#9308)

------

Video Guide: https://youtu.be/nlS4Ld8_jBc

INSTALLATION INSTRUCTIONS:

1. Have eiter SA:MP 0.3.7 R1 or 0.3.7 R3-1 installed in GTA SA v1.0. (Either SA:MP version requires a seperate version of SAMPFUNCS. Check Links.txt for more.)

2. Ensure that you have the latest versions of CLEO, ASI Loader, SAMPFUNCS and Moonloader installed. (Check Links.txt)

3. If you already have another AutoGetMats LUA mod installed in your 'moonloader' folder, remove it first to avoid conflict.

4. Copy the 'moonloader' folder from 'Bear x Adib AutoGetMats v1.8 Revised' to your GTA SA game directory. Replace existing files with new ones if necessary.

Contact Swapnil#9308 on Discord for support.

------

COMMANDS:

/autogm, gmmenu
